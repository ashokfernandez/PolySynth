What is this?

Audio samples generated directly from the C++ DSP engine during CI builds. These verify that our oscillators, filters, and envelopes are working correctly.

How to use

Click play on any sample to hear it. Listen for clean audio without clicks, pops, or distortion. Each demo tests a specific DSP component.

Technical details

All samples are rendered at 44.1kHz, 16-bit. The synth engine runs headless without any UI or DAW dependencies.
